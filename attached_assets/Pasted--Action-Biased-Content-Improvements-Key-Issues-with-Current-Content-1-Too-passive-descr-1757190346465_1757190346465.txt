## üí° KEY IMPROVEMENTS SUMMARY:

1. **Specific Dollar Amounts** - Instead of ‚Äúmeasurable growth‚Äù ‚Üí ‚Äú$2M+ growth in 90 days‚Äù
1. **Time-Bound Outcomes** - ‚Äú90-Day Recovery System‚Äù vs generic solutions
1. **Urgency Elements** - Limited spots, time-sensitive offers, monthly costs
1. **Diagnostic Tools** - Self-assessment checklist creates engagement
1. **Social Proof Enhanced** - Before/after stories with emotional context
1. **Risk Reversal** - Free audit, no commitment, guaranteed insights
1. **Problem Amplification** - Show the real cost of inaction
1. **Action-Oriented CTAs** - ‚ÄúClaim,‚Äù ‚ÄúGet,‚Äù ‚ÄúStop‚Äù vs ‚ÄúLearn More‚Äù

## üîÑ Implementation Priority:

1. **Hero Section** (highest impact)
1. **CTA Section** (conversion critical)
1. **Problem Section** (urgency building)
1. **Case Studies** (social proof)
1. **Solution Section** (differentiation)

This creates a much more compelling, action-oriented experience that drives immediate engagement rather than passive browsing.
